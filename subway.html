SUBWAY STATION




Abstract

This project served as my introduction to 3D computer graphics.
The scene is based on the subway station U Rathaus Reinickendorf in Berlin, though it is not an exact replica.

It is rendered in Unity and features 35 assets, some of which share the same model but sport different textures.

The goal of this project was to produce a selection of assets that may be arranged in a modular fashion in order to quickly construct a train station with a desired layout.

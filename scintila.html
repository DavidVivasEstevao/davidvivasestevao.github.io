SCINTILA



Abstract

Scintila is an Endless Runner Game that demands spatial awareness and the ability to plan ahead.

The player is tasked to rotate cubes around each other in order to lay out a continuous line of fuses.

Arranging a path for the spark to burn is a challenge already, but aiming for each cube’s maximum amount of points at the same time is the real deal!

FOOMP



Abstract

A soothing experience of flinging a jelly-like blob through a serene level, that serves as a playground rather than a challenge.

The player is free to color the surroundings to their liking while bouncing around slopes and squeezing through narrow gaps. The mostly organic level structure, visually inspired by the chemical process of reaction diffusion, lends itself to the flexibility of the Blobâ€™s shape.

FOOMP aims at providing essential downtime from busy schedules and stressful experiences.
